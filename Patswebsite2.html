Power Rankings: NHL 2023-2024 Season

Another NHL season is just around the corner and players are getting ready to lace them up again. Every year The Hockey Times ranks each team based off current standing, offseason aquisitions and drafts. This years list is riddled with high-end talent, But only one is primed to hoist Lord Stanley's Cup. Below are rankings for the upcoming season.

Rankings:

  • 1. Toronto Maple Leafs

    • This one should be a no-brainer at #1. Being a Leafs fan im always confident in the boys and that this "might be the year" that they actually get it done. But based off the offseason acquisitions of Bertuzzi, Domi, Klingberg, and Reaves, this team looks much deeper throughout the lineup adding grit and scoring to help push them over the hump. Locking up Auston Matthews for another 4 years was a massive step in the right direction. He's a top 3 player in the league and quite possibly the most elite goal-scorer the league has ever seen. His new contract starts at the end of this season at 4 years/13.25 million AAV. You're next Willy.
